About Me

CONTACT ME - SEND ME AN EMAIL

Hello there, my name is Shreyas Singh, and I am a proud student of S K Somaiya College . As a BAMCJ Honours student, I have always been passionate about recording and snapping images in a creative way, which helped me discover photography as my creative outlet.

Photography and Videography is freedom for me, the ability to create, make, present whatever my mind and heart desires. The thought that my hobby could be my work, pay me as well while I enjoy every bit of it just makes my heart race. The liking for photography first started just out of a curiosity when the younger me started using my dad's simple point and shoot camera, I slowly understood it and just couldn't stop capturing people, things and moments.

When i got my Mobile phone it just kept growing further. As for videography, when i grew a little older, while watching movies, series and other stuff on tv in general i felt like I could have made that particular scene or shot better, I took my phone switched to video and the rest is history, I recorded all the things that i found attractive or had meaning. And so my love for direction also grew.

Videography

In addition to photography, I have also discovered a passion for videography. Through this medium, I am able to tell even more compelling stories by combining visuals, sound, and motion. With videography, I am able to capture a moment in time and bring it to life in a way that photography cannot. Whether it's a breathtaking landscape, a dynamic event, or an intimate conversation, I strive to create videos that not only capture the moment but also transport the viewer to that time and place.
